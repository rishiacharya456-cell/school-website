About Saraswoti English Boarding School

Introduction

Saraswoti English Boarding School (SEBS) is a leading co-educational institution dedicated to academic excellence and holistic development of students.

With a balanced blend of modern teaching methods and proven educational practices, the school promotes all-round development—intellectual, moral, social, emotional, and physical.

Guided by the four pillars of education—Learning to Know, Learning to Do, Learning to Live Together, and Learning to Be—the school ensures meaningful and lifelong learning.

Situated in a peaceful, green, and pollution-free environment, Saraswoti English Boarding School provides a safe and learner-friendly atmosphere.

With well-managed infrastructure and value-based education, SEBS is an ideal choice for quality education and character building.
