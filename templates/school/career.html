Principal's Message

Suresh Thapa

Principal, SEBS

“Learning starts in infancy, long before formal education begins and continues throughout life.”

I extend a warm welcome to you and your family to Saraswoti English Boarding School.

It is my great pleasure to welcome you to Saraswoti English Boarding School. Our school is committed to providing quality education that nurtures academic excellence, creativity, values, and confidence in every child. We believe education is not only about gaining knowledge but also about developing character, imagination, and lifelong learning skills.

At SEBS, we focus on student-centered learning through a balanced curriculum, dedicated teachers, and a safe, caring environment. We encourage creativity, critical thinking, and active participation, especially through play-based and experiential learning in the early years. The well-being and holistic development of our students remain our top priority.

We highly value the role of parents as partners in education and believe that strong parent–teacher collaboration leads to students’ success. Together, we strive to build a vibrant learning community where every child can discover their potential and grow into a responsible and confident individual.

Let us work together to shape a bright and meaningful future for our children.

“Let noble thoughts come to us from every side.”
